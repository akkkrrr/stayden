Every stay-up wearer knows the feeling: that slow, inevitable slide down the thigh that turns confidence into anxiety. The silicone band that promised to hold gives up halfway through dinner. The constant, invisible battle between gravity and grip.

We tested over two dozen stay-ups across three months, multiple body types, and real-world conditions — not a photoshoot. Here are the seven that actually earned their name.

## Our Testing Criteria

Every stay-up was evaluated on five factors: silicone grip strength, comfort over 8+ hours, denier consistency, durability across washes, and price-to-quality ratio. We wore them to offices, dinners, events, and everywhere in between.

## 1. Wolford Individual 10 Stay-Up

The gold standard. Wolford's Individual 10 delivers an almost invisible sheer with a silicone band that genuinely works. The secret is the wider band — more surface area means better grip without cutting into skin. Premium price, but the experience justifies every cent.

**Denier:** 10 | **Price:** ~€39 | **Grip:** Excellent | **Durability:** Very Good

## 2. Falke Shelina 12 Stay-Up

Falke's answer to ultra-sheer perfection. The Shelina 12 has a matt finish that photographs beautifully and a silicone band with a lace pattern that doubles as decoration. Slightly more affordable than Wolford, arguably just as good.

**Denier:** 12 | **Price:** ~€28 | **Grip:** Excellent | **Durability:** Good

## 3. Pretty Polly Nylons 10 Lace Top

The budget champion. At under €12, these deliver surprisingly reliable grip and a classic lace-top aesthetic. They are not as durable as premium options — expect 3-5 wears rather than 10+ — but the value is undeniable.

**Denier:** 10 | **Price:** ~€12 | **Grip:** Good | **Durability:** Fair

## 4. Bluebella Lace Top Hold Ups

When you want your stay-ups to be part of the outfit, not hidden under it. Bluebella's lace detailing is gorgeous, the silicone is strong, and the denier range offers options from sheer to opaque.

**Denier:** 15-20 | **Price:** ~€18 | **Grip:** Very Good | **Durability:** Good

## 5. Kunert Satin Look 20

The sleeper hit. Kunert does not get enough attention outside Europe, but their Satin Look 20 has a beautiful glossy finish and one of the most comfortable silicone bands we tested. A genuine discovery.

**Denier:** 20 | **Price:** ~€15 | **Grip:** Very Good | **Durability:** Very Good

## 6. Wolford Satin Touch 20

If the Individual 10 is Wolford's sheer champion, the Satin Touch is its all-rounder. A subtle satin sheen, a robust silicone band, and a 20-denier weight that works across seasons.

**Denier:** 20 | **Price:** ~€35 | **Grip:** Excellent | **Durability:** Excellent

## 7. Calzedonia 15 Denier Stay-Up

The accessible option. Available on every high street, Calzedonia's 15-denier stay-up offers decent grip and a good color range. Perfect for testing whether stay-ups are for you before investing in premium.

**Denier:** 15 | **Price:** ~€10 | **Grip:** Fair-Good | **Durability:** Fair

## The Verdict

**Unlimited budget:** Wolford Individual 10 or Satin Touch 20. Worth every cent.
**Best value:** Falke Shelina 12 — premium feel without the premium price.
**First time trying stay-ups:** Pretty Polly or Calzedonia — low risk, decent quality.
